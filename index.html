About ISM

Independent Study & Mentorship (ISM) is a rigorous course offered to FISD Juniors and Seniors. Students learn essential skills such as public speaking, research analysis, and product design. Throughout the course, student s conduct interviews, connect with professionals, and research a topic. Under a mentor, students then create their original work and final product.
